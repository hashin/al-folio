Suggestions

  1. Francis Bacon on Stanford's Plato

  16. Anything but Straight: Unmasking the Scandals and Lies Behind the Ex-Gay Myth by Wayne Besen
  17. The Imitation Game: Alan Turing Decoded (Graphic Novel) by Jim Ottaviani
  18. Enigma: How the Poles Broke the Nazi Code by Wladyslaw Kozaczuk and Jerzy Straszak
